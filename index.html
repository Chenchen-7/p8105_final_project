Overview

This project analyzes New York City Citi Bike usage patterns during 2024 (especially October).

Data sourse

This project uses the publicly available Citi Bike system data provided by Lyft through the official Citi Bike website: Citibike data. Weather information was obtained from the Open-Meteo Weather API: weather data.
